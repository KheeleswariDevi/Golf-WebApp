FAQ

Can I bring my dog to the golf course?

No pets are allowed on the golf course or on site.

What payment types are accepted?

We accept payment by debit card, bank transfer, cash, or cheque.

What should I wear?

You simply need to wear comfortable clothing. Thin layers are ideal; they’re easy to move in, and you can add or remove extra layers depending on the weather.

Is golf hard to learn?

Not at all. Though it certainly requires a lot of practice and playing time to reach the level you desire as a player.

Can I book over the phone?

Yes, you can, alternatively, you can book by email.

Can a guest play 9 holes on the main course?

Yes, a guest can play 9 holes, the fee is half.

Do you have rental pull trolleys?

Yes, a rental machine is located at the first tee; cost per trolley is €4.

Is there a baggage room available for golf clubs?

Yes, a baggage room is located beside the golfshop; this room is locked each night and has security cameras in operation.
